Top 5 National Parks to Visit

  1. Jim Corbett National Park, Uttrakhand
  2. Kanha National Park, Madhya Pradesh
  3. Ranthambore National Park, Rajasthan
  4. Gir National Park, Gujrat
  5. Kaziranga National Park, Assam

Essentials to carry :

  • Flashlights/Headlamp
  • Navigational Tools (Map, Compass)
  • First-Aid Kit
  • Plenty of Water and Snacks
  • Weather-Appropriate Clothing
  • Power Bank for phones
  • Binoculars
  • Backpack

Outdoor Terminology

WILDLAND
Untouched,natural landscapes
TRAIL MARKER/BLAZE
Painted symbol or sign showing the trail path.
HABITAT
Natural environment where plants/animals live.
BEAR BOX BEAR / BEAR CANISTER
A secure food storage container to keep wildlife out
